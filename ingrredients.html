Scouty Snacks Ingredients

Our Scouty Snacks are made from only the finest, all-natural ingredients. We believe in transparency and the power of simple, nutritious food. Here's what goes into our treats:

  • Whole wheat flour - A healthy base for our snacks
  • Peanut butter - For a taste all dogs love
  • Applesauce - Naturally sweet and full of fiber
  • Oats - For that perfect chewy texture

Every ingredient is carefully selected for the health and happiness of your four-legged friends!
